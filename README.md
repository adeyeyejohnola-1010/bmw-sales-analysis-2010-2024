**Introduction**

In this project, I analyzed global sales performance data for BMW from 2010 to 2024. The goal was to uncover key trends in sales growth, customer preferences, regional performance, and product demand, and to translate these findings into actionable business insights.

**Story of the Data**

The dataset captures BMW’s global operations across multiple dimensions, including:

Sales figures over time (2010–2024) 

Regional performance (Asia, Europe, North America, etc.) 

Vehicle models and their sales performance 

Fuel types (Petrol, Diesel, Hybrid, Electric) 

Customer transmission preferences (Manual vs Automatic) 

Pricing segments and color preferences 

This data tells a story of how market demand evolves across regions, technologies, and customer behavior over time

**Data Processing**

I carried out several data preparation steps to ensure accuracy and usability:

Cleaned missing and inconsistent entries 

Standardized categorical variables   

Structured the dataset for efficient dashboard reporting in Excel 

**Pre-Analysis**

Before building the dashboard, I explored the dataset to understand its structure and identify initial patterns:

Verified total sales volume and distribution across years 

Identified key variables that influence sales (region, model, fuel type)

This stage helped define the direction of the analysis and the layout of the dashboard.

**Post-Analysis and Insights**

**Regional & Market Behavior**

Asia shows strong demand for black and blue vehicles 

Africa favors 5 Series and 3 Series models 

North America sees strong performance from X1 and X3 models 

Europe maintains consistent demand across multiple models 

**Fuel & Technology Trends**

North America leads in electric vehicle adoption 

Asia and Europe follow closely in EV demand 

Hybrid adoption is increasing globally

**Fuel Type by Region**

South America leads in diesel sales 

Middle East dominates petrol vehicle demand 

Africa records the lowest diesel sales 

➡️ Overall Insight: Market preferences vary significantly by region, emphasizing the need for localized strategies.

**Data Visualization and Charts**

To communicate insights effectively, I designed an interactive Excel dashboard featuring:

KPI cards (Total Sales, Top Model, Leading Region) 

Line chart for yearly sales trend 

Donut charts for customer preference and fuel distribution 

Bar charts for model performance and regional comparison 

Slicers for dynamic filtering (e.g., color preferences) 

The dashboard allows users to explore data intuitively and make informed decisions.

**Observations**

	Sales are globally diversified but led by Asia 
	
	Hybrid vehicles are becoming the dominant fuel type 
	
	Mid-range luxury vehicles generate the most revenue 
	
	Customer preferences (transmission, color, model) vary significantly by region 
	
**Recommendations**

Strengthen marketing and dealership presence in high-performing regions like Asia and Europe
    
Increase production and promotion of top-selling models such as the 7 Series and i8
	
Customize product offerings (colors, features) based on regional preferences

Invest more in hybrid and electric vehicle development to align with future mobility trends
   
**Conclusion**

This analysis highlights BMW’s strong global positioning, driven by diversified markets, high-performing flagship models, and a growing shift toward sustainable vehicle options.

By leveraging regional insights and aligning product strategies with customer preferences, BMW can further strengthen its market leadership and adapt to the evolving automotive landscape.
